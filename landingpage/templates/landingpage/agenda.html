Day 4 — Adventure Day (10Th July)

Morning

Game Drive & Wildlife

We shall have a game drive at Nairobi National Park from 6AM to 10Am.You can also decide to have breakfast inside the park.

Optional activities:

  • The David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust (DSWT) is a renowned wildlife conservation organization in Kenya, best known for its elephant orphanage located near Nairobi National Park. It rescues and rehabilitates orphaned baby elephants and rhinos<
  • The Giraffe Centre in Lang’ata, Nairobi, is a conservation and education center dedicated to protecting the endangered Rothschild’s giraffe.
  • The Maasai Market is a vibrant open-air craft market held at various locations across Nairobi throughout the week. Feel free to pay a visit on your rest days.
